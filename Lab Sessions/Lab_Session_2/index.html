About David Goggins

David Goggins is a former Navy SEAL, Air Force Tactical Air Controller, and Army Ranger School graduate. But his story didn’t start there. He overcame poverty, racism, learning disabilities, and childhood trauma to become a living symbol of mental toughness.

Today, he’s an ultramarathon runner, endurance athlete, motivational speaker, and bestselling author. His message is simple but powerful: Get comfortable being uncomfortable. Callous your mind. Stay hard.

Major Achievements

  • Only member of the U.S. Armed Forces to complete SEAL training, U.S. Army Ranger School, and Air Force Tactical Air Control training
  • Ran 100 miles in under 24 hours — with no real training
  • Set a world record: 4,030 pull-ups in 17 hours
  • Author of *Can’t Hurt Me* and *Never Finished* — both bestsellers
